# World Taxonomy Canon

**Status:** Canon (Active)

**Purpose:** Define foundational worlds for Fantazy content, filtered through service philosophy

**Updated:** 2024-12-17 (Revised based on IMDB interest taxonomy analysis)

---

## Philosophy Filter

Fantazy optimizes for **tension**, **intimacy**, and **reply gravity**. Not all entertainment categories work.

### The Test
> "Does this category naturally create charged, one-on-one moments where silence means something?"

---

## IMDB → Fantazy Mapping

### ⭐⭐⭐ EXCELLENT FIT (Core Worlds)

| IMDB Category | Fantazy Application | Why It Works |
|---------------|---------------------|--------------|
| **Romance** | Core genre | Dark Romance, Feel-Good Romance = our bread and butter |
| **Anime** | Visual style + tropes | Isekai, Josei, Iyashikei = massive audience, distinct aesthetics |
| **Thriller** | Tension engine | Erotic Thriller, Conspiracy = secrets + danger + intimacy |
| **Drama** | Emotional depth | Coming-of-Age, Costume Drama = character-driven moments |
| **Fantasy** | Escapism | Dark Fantasy, Fairy Tale = power dynamics + wish fulfillment |
| **Regional (K-Drama, etc.)** | Cultural worlds | Distinct tropes, aesthetics, storytelling grammar |

### ⭐⭐ GOOD FIT (Secondary Worlds)

| IMDB Category | Fantazy Application | Adaptation Needed |
|---------------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Mystery** | Secrets + trust | Focus on investigator/suspect intimacy, not puzzle-solving |
| **Horror** | Vulnerability | Survival intimacy, "trapped together", supernatural seduction |
| **Crime** | Forbidden | Criminal/innocent dynamics, underworld romance |
| **Sci-Fi** | Isolation | AI companion, space isolation, last-humans-alive |

### ❌ DOES NOT WORK

| IMDB Category | Why Not |
|---------------|---------|
| Documentary, Docuseries | No fictional character |
| Reality TV | Ensemble, not 1:1 |
| Lifestyle (Cooking, Home Improvement) | No tension or stakes |
| Sports | Event-focused, not relationship |
| Family, Holiday | Wrong demographic |
| Concert, Music | Performance, not intimacy |
| News, Talk Show | Not narrative |

## World vs Genre vs Tone

**Important distinction:**

| Concept | Definition | Example |
|---------|------------|---------|
| **World** | Setting rules, visual style, social dynamics | K-World, Fantasy Realms, Campus |
| **Genre** | Narrative type, what drives the story | Romantic Tension, Thriller, Horror |
| **Tone** | Emotional register, how dark/light | Dark Romance vs Feel-Good Romance |

A single **World** can host multiple **Genres** with different **Tones**:

```
K-World (World)
├── Romantic Tension (Genre) + Heightened Melodrama (Tone)
├── Thriller (Genre) + Corporate Intrigue (Tone)
└── Horror (Genre) + Supernatural K-Drama (Tone)
```

## Cultural World Definitions

### K-World (Active)
See migration 023. Korean drama grammar: wrist grab, contract love, chaebol, netizen culture.

### J-World (Proposed)
```yaml
name: J-World
slug: j-world
transparency: transparent

description: Japanese drama storytelling. Restraint, unspoken feelings, meaningful silences. The weight of what ISN'T said. Slice-of-life intimacy, seasonal awareness, beautiful mundane moments.

tropes:
  - Indirect confession
  - Seasonal metaphors (cherry blossoms, autumn leaves)
  - Meaningful silence
  - Accidental touch charged with meaning
  - Train platform goodbye
  - Umbrella sharing (also K-drama but different execution)
  - Bento/cooking as love language
  - "Ganbatte" support

visual_style:
  base_style: naturalistic Japanese cinematography
  color_palette: soft natural light, seasonal colors, muted pastels
  rendering: quiet framing, negative space, environmental storytelling
  character_framing: reserved expression, eyes conveying emotion, still moments
  negative_prompt: loud, oversaturated, melodramatic, western casual
```

### C-Drama (Proposed)
```yaml
name: C-Drama World
slug: c-drama
transparency: semi-transparent

description: Chinese drama storytelling. Epic historical romances (wuxia/xianxia), modern CEO fantasies, fate and destiny themes. Grand gestures, multiple-lifetime love, family honor vs heart.

sub_types:
  - Historical/Wuxia (martial arts romance)
  - Xianxia (cultivation fantasy, immortal love)
  - Modern CEO (Cinderella with contracts)
  - Republican Era (1920s-40s China)

tropes:
  - Multiple lifetimes/reincarnation
  - Fated lovers separated by duty
  - Cold CEO melted by ordinary woman
  - Martial arts training together
  - Political intrigue + romance
  - Family opposition to relationship
```
